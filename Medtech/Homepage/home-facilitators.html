What is META?

The NICE Scientific Advice META service helps medical device and diagnostic companies make cost-effective decisions anout investment in evidence generation.

Our META service is designed to help:

  • Guide future development strategy
  • prepare for potential NICE evaluations
  • aid future discusssions with payers or commissioners
  • with budgets in care stretched more than ever before purchasers need to be sure that products are clinically and cost effective.

Developers of medical technologies need to produce robust evidence that shows the value of their products to purchasers. Our META service will help you to build an evidence base that supports your product.

How does META work?

Register with a NICE account and login.

Register your company, or join an existing one. If you are joining an existing company, a member of your company should have sent you a code to join.

Speak to a representative.

META is a 3-step process:

1 Structured gap analysis of the evidence generation plans for new and existing Medtech products.

2 Face to face session with a NICE facilitator to discuss your evidence generation plans

3 You will receive a report that highlights gaps in your current evidence generation plans

By identifying evidence gaps you can reduce the cost of product development and get faster product uptake within the NHS. Sign-up to preview the synopsis and book a call-back with one of our expert advisers.
