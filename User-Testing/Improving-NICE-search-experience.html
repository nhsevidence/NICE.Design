Improving NICE search experience

November 2019

We're developing new ways to search and find information on the NICE website. Your feedback ensures we design digital services that meet user needs.

The team in digital services have some ideas about how we want to improve the search experience, but before releasing these features, we need feedback from you.

For the purpose of this activity, please consider yourself a diabetes specialist and select a scenario that's most relevant to you.

Scenario 1
As part of your professional development, you visit the NICE website to improve your understanding of diabetes. You search the NICE website and want to find information on the search result page.
