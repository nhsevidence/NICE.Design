Quality statements give you priority areas for quality improvement.

Find statements tailored to your:

  • Service
  • Setting
  • Population of interest.

How it works:

  • Select filters to target your specific area of interest.
  • Click one of the results to access the full quality statement.

Quality statements cover:

  • Areas where there is variation in care
  • Topics across health and social care.

They also include information that that tells you how to measure progress against the statement.
