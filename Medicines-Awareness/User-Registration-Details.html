Subscribe to Medicines awareness service

Register

Sign-up a daily or weekly email service that provides links to current awareness and evidence-based information relating to medicines and prescribing.

Includes a quick overview of the latest evidence-based information, to help busy health professionals stay up to date.

If this is the first time you are visiting this page, please complete the following fields and click 'Save'.

You will then be sent an email containing a link which will allow you to activate your registration. You can then login to personalise your Medicines Awareness Service emails by selecting the 'Subscription preferences' tab, which will appear in the above menu.

If you have already registered with the Medicines Awareness Service you will be able to manage and save your subscription preferences now.
