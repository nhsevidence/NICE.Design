Media campaigns

This intervention is not from NICE guidance. The main components of the intervention are as follows:

  • Participants are sent emails containing tips on how to achieve 30 minutes of moderate physical activity per day, 5 days a week
  • Regular email surveys regarding level of physical activity, motivational readiness and self efficacy sent for the first 4 weeks, monthly for 3 months and bi-monthly for 6 months
  • Participants mailed in physical activity logs and brief surveys each month for 12 months. The return of each survey was reinforced with a $10 incentive.
  • Feedback forms based on survey results were generated containing theory based counselling messages and mailed to participants.
