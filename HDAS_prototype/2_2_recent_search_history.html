Here you can build or review a complex search strategy and click on ‘View results’ when you’re ready.

 

What’s new?

 

•     You can edit searches (search rows) within the strategy (there is an ‘Edit’ button on each row)

•     You can sort your search strategy so that your latest search is at the top, or at the bottom, whichever you find most practical.

 

Additional functionality

 

Functionality that’s not yet included in this design, or not obvious from it, includes:

•     You’ll be able to add searches and group them with related searches that you entered much earlier, so as to preserve the logic of your search strategy!

 

(Note that this may not be an exhaustive list.)

 

 

 

**Don’t forget*

This is an interactive design, not actual working software. It does not perform real searches.

This is a work in progress: some of the functionality isn’t included yet, but we’re not forgetting about it.
