Welcome to the HDAS prototype

We are working to improve HDAS, specifically its performance and stability. The project is a joint venture between NICE and Health Education England (HEE). We are taking this opportunity to collect feedback from users and look at other things we might be able to improve as well.

 

Most of the work will happen behind the scenes, as we’ve decided to rewrite the whole code and structure to deliver a faster and more reliable service.

 

We have also been working hard on improved functionality. We’d like to present you our new design, and would really appreciate your feedback.

 

Please note that this is an interactive design, not actual working software. This means that, though some buttons, links and other interactive elements do work, in that something happens when you click on them, they only take you to a mock-up of what will happen on-screen when you perform that interaction; actual searches aren’t yet being performed behind the scenes. This is also a work-in-progress: some of the functionality isn’t included yet, but we’re not forgetting about it!

 

Please be aware of the following points:

 

1. Where a button, tick box or link is shown multiple times on the screen, only the top most will be interactive on the design.

 

2. This prototype does not use any real data. Everything is pre-defined to give an impression of what would happen.

 

3. This prototype is intended to demonstrate functionality only

 

4. The look and feel are not the final version, and are likely to change. We’re not collecting feedback about these aspects yet. (By look and feel we mean font, colours, exact positions of the controls, etc.)

 

5. We welcome your feedback on the functionality and modes of interactions.

 

6. Labels and terminology may also change.
